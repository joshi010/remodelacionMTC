About

Metacuki is a journey of one web developer student to success. Every small project made here is made just for fun and to practice learned lessons. As you could already tell, my projects are not complex and fancy as someone would expect them to be, but it is just a matter of time for them to be all of that. I will be uploading more and more projects over time and they will be getting better and more useful. The goal of this website is to see my progress and hopefully someday help one of you in some everyday tasks.

NAME HISTORY

>

The name METACUKI was born from a friend's nickname "cookie" and over time our friend group started to modify it, it could be by the way we pronounced it, or the way we spelled it. That's when I started to spell it "cuki". During that time NFT's where at their boom and a lot of people including myself tought that they were garbage. So I used my knowledge on web pages at the time and created an NFT collection featuring CUKI to make fun of them and showing everyone that even a random dude could make one. If you were wondering, yes it is the same CUKI I told you about. I thought that by including "META" at the begining it will give the collection a little more emphasis to the whole NFT/Metaverse stuff. That's when METACUKI was born.

MORE

>

Obviously the collection was a faliure and no one bought even one. That's fine considering that it was made just for fun, and fun was what I had the most. It was the first "serious" webpage that I ever did, and I even learned how to use photoshop to make all of cuki's edits. Overall it was a good experience, but because I had already bought the domain name metacuki.com I decided to use it to upload some of my projects.

Disclaimer: I'm not CUKI, I'm one of his friends that decided to use his image with his consent to make a "brand". I'm not using it to make money off of him in any way. You may only use his photos if it is to make a meme (It must make him laugh).
